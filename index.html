About Us

Our Trust was started under the Inspiration of Vivekanandar's Humanity and Teachings. Vivekanandar Trust is a social service organisation started in MAY 2000 (01-05-2000). Our Trust is Registered Charitable Trust, registered in the year 2007 under the Indian Trust Act,TamilNadu bearing the registration number 2683/2007.It was started under the Inspiration of Vivekanandar's Humanity thoughts and teachings. The service offered by this Trust are common to all irrespective of caste,race,religion and language.

Mission

❤ To take care old aged destitute persons.
❤ Giving Shelter,Food,Clothes,Medicine and Mother Care to the old aged persons.
❤ Find Funerals after their life ends.
❤ Serving the needy persons.

Vision

❤ Timely Care and making access for better nutrition,health and social living until their last needs.
❤ Paying attention on the welfare of the aged with special care.
❤ Carrying out Other Welfare programs with local support.

Services

Our Vivekanandar Illam's main aim is make people to feel as this home.
High Quality Breakfast,Lunch,Snacks and Dinner are available in time.
Special Homecare is given to each individuals.
Regular Medical Checkups are conducted by Experienced Doctors at Door Step.

All the above Services are done free of cost.

Volunteers can also offer or donate food for their Birthdays, Wedding Anniversaries, Special days, Dhevasham(Theethi). We will pray for your wellness on that day. You can come and visit our home and serve food yourself. The tariff for food are given below..
